Sevari

The work behind your
business runs itself

Sevari executes the operational work behind a business or individual project — building, marketing, selling, supporting, and maintaining — using a structured system of specialist agents. You define the direction. The system handles the work.

What Sevari runs

Build

Writes and maintains code, deploys systems.

Market

Creates content and runs campaigns.

Sell

Executes outreach and manages leads.

Support

Handles inbound queries and responses.

Finance

Generates invoices and tracks revenue.

Operate

Monitors systems and performance.

How it works

Tasks enter through a structured pipeline. Each task is scanned, verified, routed to a specialist agent, executed with a failsafe chain, and passed through approval gates before delivery.

Intake → Guardian → Credentials → Agent → Failsafe → Approval → Audit → Learning

You remain in control

Failure is controlled

Sevari uses a six-tier execution chain. If one model fails, another takes over. If all AI fails, deterministic templates execute. If required, tasks are escalated.

Final fallback: structured templates — zero AI, guaranteed output.
