What Serenity is

Serenity is a deterministic enquiry processing engine designed for businesses. Every enquiry follows a structured pipeline — ensuring no loss, no inconsistency, and complete traceability.

How it works

Receive → Validate → Classify → Enrich → Respond → Audit → Log

Every enquiry entering the system is processed through a fixed execution path. Nothing is skipped. Nothing is lost.

Completion is enforced

Serenity uses a multi-layer failsafe chain. If AI fails, fallback systems execute. If all AI fails, deterministic templates ensure completion.

Final fallback: structured templates — zero AI dependency, guaranteed output.
